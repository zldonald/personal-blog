## Competitive Landscape

### Incumbent Players
Large, established companies with:
- Distribution advantages
- Feature breadth
- Enterprise relationships

**Our angle**: They're built for the average case. We're built for the exceptional case.

### Emerging Competitors
Venture-backed startups with:
- More capital
- Aggressive growth tactics
- Feature parity strategies

**Our angle**: They're racing to the middle. We're staying on the edge.

### Our Position
We occupy a distinct position:

| Moat | Distribution | Capital | Taste |

## Timing

Why now?

1. **AI commoditization** — as AI tools become ubiquitous, human judgment becomes scarce
2. **Quality backlash** — users increasingly reject generic, optimized experiences
3. **Market maturation** — buyers can distinguish between noise and signal
