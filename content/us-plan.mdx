## Market Entry Strategy

### Phase 1: Beachhead (Months 1-6)

Establish presence in a single metro area with high concentration of target customers:

- **Location**: San Francisco / Bay Area
- **Focus**: Build density, not breadth
- **Goal**: 50 high-signal customers

### Phase 2: Proof Points (Months 6-12)

Demonstrate repeatability:

- Expand to second market (NYC or LA)
- Document playbook from Phase 1
- Hire first US-based team members

### Phase 3: Scale (Year 2)

With proven model:

- Accelerate geographic expansion
- Build US headquarters
- Establish enterprise motion

<Callout type="insight">
Speed follows certainty. We move fast once we know what's working.
</Callout>
